[h1]Star Trek Modules - Industrial Replicator[/h1]

[b]A powerful industrial-scale replicator system with LCARS interface for your Star Trek server![/b]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]📋 Description[/h2]

The Industrial Replicator is an advanced addon for Star Trek Modules that provides a spawnable entity capable of replicating a wide variety of props, equipment, and supplies. Perfect for outfitting your starship, station, or away team with essential gear!

Unlike standard replicators, the Industrial Replicator features a fixed output location, making it ideal for automated supply chains and organized cargo operations.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]✨ Features[/h2]

[list]
[*] [b]Full LCARS Interface[/b] - Authentic Star Trek-styled interface that spawns on a plate model
[*] [b]Fixed Replication Point[/b] - All items materialize at a preset location for organized logistics
[*] [b]10-Second Cooldown[/b] - Prevents spam and adds realism to replication operations
[*] [b]22 Replicatable Items[/b] - Organized into 6 categories for easy access
[*] [b]Cooldown Feedback[/b] - Visual display shows remaining cooldown time
[*] [b]Fully Integrated[/b] - Works seamlessly with Star Trek Modules framework
[/list]

[h2]🎮 How to Use[/h2]

[olist]
[*] Spawn the [b]Industrial Replicator[/b] entity from the Star Trek category in your spawn menu
[*] Position it where you want the control panel to be
[*] Press [b]E (Use)[/b] on the entity to open the LCARS interface
[*] Select a category from the menu
[*] Choose an item to replicate
[*] The item will materialize at the fixed replication coordinates
[*] Wait 10 seconds before replicating the next item
[/olist]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]⚙️ Configuration[/h2]

The replication coordinates are set to:
[code]
Position: x=291.29, y=61.04, z=11714.17
Angle: p=0.02, y=179.99, r=-0.00
[/code]

[i]Note: These coordinates can be modified in the entity's init.lua file if needed for your specific map layout.[/i]
